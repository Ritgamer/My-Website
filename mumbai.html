Visitor Care Tips

  • Emergency Contacts: Dial 100 for police, 102 for medical emergencies, and 101 for fire.
  • Local Transport: Use prepaid taxis or trusted ride-sharing apps like Uber and Ola.
  • Health & Safety: Always drink bottled water and avoid street food if unaccustomed to local cuisine.
  • Currency Exchange: Exchange money at authorized centers for the best rates.
  • Hotel Assistance: Contact the hotel concierge for any travel guidance or emergency help.

Must-Visit Places in Mumbai

  • Gateway of India: An iconic landmark overlooking the Arabian Sea, a must-visit for history lovers.
  • Marine Drive: A scenic boulevard ideal for evening walks and stunning sunset views.
  • Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus: A UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its breathtaking Victorian Gothic architecture.
  • Elephanta Caves: Ancient rock-cut caves with exquisite sculptures dedicated to Lord Shiva.
  • Juhu Beach: A lively beach destination famous for street food and Bollywood celebrity sightings.
  • Haji Ali Dargah: A stunning mosque and tomb located on an islet in the Arabian Sea.
