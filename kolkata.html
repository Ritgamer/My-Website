Visitor Care Tips

  • Carry a copy of your identification and important travel documents.
  • Use only registered taxi services or ride-sharing apps for safe transportation.
  • Stay hydrated and avoid drinking tap water.
  • Respect local customs and dress modestly when visiting religious places.
  • Be cautious of pickpockets in crowded areas.

Top Visiting Places in Kolkata

  • Victoria Memorial - A stunning marble structure dedicated to Queen Victoria.
  • Howrah Bridge - An iconic bridge and one of Kolkata's landmarks.
  • Indian Museum - The oldest and largest museum in India.
  • Park Street - Famous for its nightlife, restaurants, and colonial charm.
  • Belur Math - A serene temple dedicated to Swami Vivekananda.
