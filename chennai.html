Visitor Care Tips

  • Carry a copy of your identification and important travel documents.
  • Use only registered taxi services or ride-sharing apps for safe transportation.
  • Stay hydrated and avoid drinking tap water.
  • Respect local customs and dress modestly when visiting religious places.
  • Be cautious of pickpockets in crowded areas.

Top Visiting Places in Chennai

  • Marina Beach - The longest natural urban beach in India.
  • Kapaleeshwarar Temple - A stunning example of Dravidian architecture.
  • Fort St. George - A historical landmark from the colonial era.
  • San Thome Basilica - A beautiful church built over St. Thomas' tomb.
  • Valluvar Kottam - A grand monument dedicated to Tamil poet Thiruvalluvar.
