Visitor Care Tips

  • Emergency Contacts: Dial 100 for police, 102 for medical emergencies, and 101 for fire.
  • Local Transport: Use Metro, auto-rickshaws, or ride-sharing apps like Uber and Ola.
  • Health & Safety: Always drink bottled water and be cautious when consuming street food.
  • Currency Exchange: Exchange money at authorized centers for the best rates.
  • Hotel Assistance: Contact the hotel concierge for any travel guidance or emergency help.

Must-Visit Places in Bangalore

  • Lalbagh Botanical Garden: A beautiful garden with rare plant species and a famous glasshouse.
  • Bangalore Palace: A historic palace showcasing Tudor-style architecture.
  • ISKCON Temple: A grand temple dedicated to Lord Krishna, known for its serene atmosphere.
  • Cubbon Park: A lush green space ideal for nature lovers and morning joggers.
  • MG Road: A bustling shopping and entertainment hub in the city.
  • Nandi Hills: A scenic getaway spot known for its breathtaking sunrise views.
